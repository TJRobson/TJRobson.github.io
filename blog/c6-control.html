  • Chapter 5 - Search Inside Yourself.
  • When programming, what are some of your triggers? What causes a disproportionate emotional response?

  • I’m most angry when something seems like it should be working and isn’t. I feel that it’s as if “it’s” doing it to spite me, which is totally irrational. I also get a feeling of being entertained, though..

  • How did you find the Siberian North Railroad exercise (Stop, Breathe, Notice, Reflect, Respond)?

  • I found this both easy and hard. It was hard because I tend to release my anger nearer to the time in which it occurred, so it was hard to channel from long past events. The easy part was that once I did, the steps achieved the desired result. Once the process had been completed, it was very easy to focus on other things while maintaining a neutral position.
